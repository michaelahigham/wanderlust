31.4 milion

Dynamic cities with luxurious malls, idyllic islands, bustling nightmarketss and bountiful national parks - Malaysia has it all. Consisting of two regions separated by the South China Sea, this multi-ethnic, multi-religious country should make it on to your to-visit list.

Whether you visit the capitol Kuala Lumpur, explore Penang (formerly known as the “Pearl of the Orient”), step back in time in Malacca or descend on the tranquil Langkawi - there is a place that will appeal to all. You can enjoy Malaysia on a budget or by splashing cash.

Malaysia is also a great launching-pad to other countries in South-East Asia, including Singapore and Thailand. The best time to visit Malaysia is between March & September.
